AE Monogram — Identity Mark v2

The Horizontal Anchor

The AE Monogram is anchored by a singular, uninterrupted horizontal stroke. This shared crossbar represents the "unyielding horizon" of luxury real estate—bridging the gap between architectural precision (the 'A') and the elegance of high-end estate management (the 'E'). This element must remain consistently rendered in Primary Red (#e11d48) regardless of background context.

01 / Minimum Sizes

24PX DIGITAL
8MM PRINT

02 / Clear Space

To maintain visual integrity, the monogram must be surrounded by a clear space zone equivalent to the width of the 'E' arm (36 units in the coordinate system). No other graphical elements should infringe upon this perimeter.
